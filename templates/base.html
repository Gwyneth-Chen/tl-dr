Lega-lies

We've all seen it: huge blocks of dense text that pop up before you install a new piece of software. These days, End User License Agreements are everywhere but most of the time they are too long and full of jargon for consumers to feasibly read. But no more! Our application will find the most important parts of legal document and summarize them. Together we can make sure that people are not inadvertently agreeing to dubious privacy violations and signing away their rights. Click below to see some sample EULAs that we summarized using our app!
